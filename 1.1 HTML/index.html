Ukrainian Borscht Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large beets, peeled and grated
  • 4 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream and fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and grated carrots. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated beets to the pot. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the beets start to soften.
  3. Pour in the beef or vegetable broth. Add diced potatoes, tomato paste, bay leaf, white vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. Remove the bay leaf from the pot before serving.
  7. Ladle the borscht into bowls and garnish each serving with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of fresh dill.
  8. Serve hot with crusty bread or sourdough for a delicious Ukrainian meal. Enjoy!
